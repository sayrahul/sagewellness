Hormone Harmony Program

The SAGE Hormone Harmony Program is designed to address hormonal imbalances during peri-menopause, menopause, and post-menopause, offering comprehensive support and guidance. Participants undergo a 5-day guided detox aimed at boosting hormone health, followed by personalized hormone balance therapies and nutrition plans. Additionally, the program includes 121 knowledge-sharing sessions to empower individuals with valuable insights and strategies. Participants also receive nourishing recipes, shopping guides, and stress management tools to promote overall well-being and hormonal harmony.

F.A.Q's

Get answers to commonly asked questions about the SAGE Hormone Harmony Program

Who is this program designed for?

This program is designed for women experiencing peri-menopause, menopause, and post-menopause hormonal imbalances.

What does the 5-day guided detox entail?

The detox involves a structured regimen to support hormone health through dietary and lifestyle interventions, aimed at rebalancing hormones naturally.

How are hormone balance therapies personalized?

Therapies are personalized based on individual health needs and hormone levels, incorporating naturopathic treatments and Ayurvedic principles to address specific imbalances.

Can you provide more details about the customized nutrition plans?

Nutrition plans are tailored to address specific hormonal imbalances, emphasizing whole foods, balanced macronutrients, and hormone-supportive ingredients to promote optimal health.

What topics are covered in the 121 knowledge-sharing sessions?

The sessions cover various topics related to hormone health, including understanding hormonal changes, managing symptoms, optimizing nutrition, and lifestyle factors for hormonal balance.

How comprehensive is the hormone balance support?

Hormone balance support is comprehensive, encompassing both physical and emotional aspects of well-being, with personalized guidance and ongoing monitoring to ensure optimal results.

What information is included in the shopping guides?

Shopping guides provide information on hormone-supportive foods, supplements, and lifestyle products, making it easy for participants to make informed choices and support their hormone health.

How do the stress management tools help participants?

Stress management tools offered include relaxation techniques, mindfulness practices, and stress-reducing activities, helping participants manage stress effectively to support hormonal balance and overall well-being.

How does this program promote holistic health?

The program promotes holistic health by addressing hormonal imbalances from a multi-faceted approach, integrating nutrition, lifestyle modifications, stress management, and supportive therapies to optimize overall well-being and hormonal harmony.

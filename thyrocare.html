SAGE Thyrocare Program

Benifits of Programs

The SAGE Thyrocare Program offers a comprehensive approach to managing and reversing thyroid disorders. By incorporating natural detox methods, anti-inflammatory diets, herbal protocols, and mind-body practices, participants can achieve balanced thyroid levels, improved weight management, and overall better health. This holistic program supports long-term wellness by addressing the underlying causes of thyroid dysfunction.

F.A.Q's

Get answers to commonly asked questions about the SAGE Thyrocare Program

What thyroid disorders does the program address?

The program addresses both hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism.

What is the Green Blood Detox?

The Green Blood Detox is a natural detoxification process that uses chlorophyll-rich foods to cleanse and rejuvenate the body.

How does the Anti-Inflammatory Diet Plan help?

The Anti-Inflammatory Diet Plan reduces inflammation in the body, which can improve thyroid function and overall health.

What is the Herbology Protocol?

The Herbology Protocol involves the use of specific herbs known to support thyroid health and balance hormone levels.

What are Chakra Meditations?

Chakra Meditations are guided meditations that focus on balancing the body's energy centers to promote physical and emotional healing.

How does Yoga Therapy benefit thyroid health?

Yoga Therapy includes specific poses and breathing exercises designed to stimulate the thyroid gland and improve its function.

Is the treatment plan personalized?

Yes, each participant receives a customized treatment plan tailored to their specific thyroid condition and health needs.

Can this program help with weight management?

Yes, the program includes weight management strategies to help participants achieve and maintain a healthy weight.

How often are health assessments conducted?

Regular health assessments are conducted to monitor progress and adjust the treatment plan as needed.

What kind of support is provided during the program?

Participants receive ongoing support from our team of specialists, including nutritional guidance, stress reduction techniques, and regular health monitoring.

F.A.Q's

Get answers to commonly asked questions about the SAGE Boost Fertility Program

Is the SAGE Boost Fertility Program suitable for individuals with specific fertility challenges?

Yes, the program is designed to address a variety of fertility challenges, including PCOD, poor egg quality, fibroids, advanced age, thyroid issues, and hormonal imbalances.

How long does the Boost Fertility Program typically last?

The duration of the program varies depending on individual needs and goals. Our team will work with you to create a customized plan tailored to your specific situation.

Can the Boost Fertility Program be combined with other fertility treatments or medications?

Our program can complement other fertility treatments or medications, but we recommend consulting with your healthcare provider to ensure compatibility and safety.

What dietary and lifestyle changes are recommended as part of the Boost Fertility Program?

The program includes weekly action plans and guidance on fertility-boosting foods, as well as recommendations for meditation and yoga to support overall well-being and fertility.

How soon can I expect to see results from the Boost Fertility Program?

Results may vary depending on individual circumstances, but many participants experience improvements in fertility health and overall well-being within a few months of starting the program.

Are there any specific age requirements or restrictions for participating in the Boost Fertility Program?

The program is suitable for individuals of all ages who are looking to enhance their fertility health naturally. Our team will assess your individual needs and tailor the program accordingly.

Is the Boost Fertility Program available remotely?

Yes, we offer remote consultations and virtual program options to accommodate clients who are unable to visit our physical location.

Are there any dietary restrictions or special considerations to be aware of during the Boost Fertility Program?

Our team will provide guidance on dietary modifications and lifestyle changes tailored to your specific needs and goals. We'll work with you to address any dietary restrictions or concerns you may have.

Can the Boost Fertility Program be customized to accommodate individual preferences and circumstances?

Yes, the program is highly customizable to accommodate your unique preferences, circumstances, and fertility goals. Our team will work closely with you to develop a personalized plan that meets your needs.

How can I get started with the Boost Fertility Program?

To get started, simply contact us to schedule an initial consultation. During this consultation, we'll discuss your fertility goals and develop a personalized plan to help you achieve them.
